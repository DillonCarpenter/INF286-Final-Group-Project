Personalization


We offer a service to emboss a variety of leather goods with names or monograms (see tray of brass letters for embossing below):

  • Bibles
  • Brief Cases
  • Portfolios
  • Wallets
  • Money Clips
  • Belts
  • Guitar Straps
  • and more...


For company logos and other artwork, we can have a die made. There is an extra charge for this service.

Some vinyl and other materials can also be embossed. Please check with our bookbinders for more information.
