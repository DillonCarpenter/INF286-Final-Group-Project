About OBS Bindery and Repair

In business since 1940, we have three bookbinders experienced in the restoration and binding of books. As a used and rare bookstore with over 300,000 volumes in stock, we have 70 years experience and understand the way old books should be bound.

We specialize in custom binding, which offers you a wide variety of cover materials from which to choose. Options range from leather, imitation leather, linen and library buckram, all in a wide range of colors.
